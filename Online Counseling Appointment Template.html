FAQ’s

    What is Cranium Café?


    The real-time Student Services software was built to simulate an in-office appointment specifically designed to extend the reach to all off campus students. The café is a user friendly meeting and collaboration platform that requires no download or installation. Students “knock” on the door during a scheduled appointment with a counselor and receive immediate help regardless of location or ability.
  1. Can I use Cranium Café anywhere?
    Yes, you are able to access Cranium Café anywhere with internet access and a compatible device & browser. It is recommended that you use the café in a quiet and private environment. Please review the full technical requirements listed below for more information.
  2. I have a MAC, can I still use Cranium Café?
    Please use either a Chrome or Firefox internet browser for all video chat appointments on a MAC Desktop or Laptop. Unfortunately, Apple products such as iPhone and iPad are not compatible with Cranium Café and cannot be used for video chat appointments.
  3. Is Cranium Café FERPA and ADA Compliant?
    Cranium Café is Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) compliant. Users can also utilize a closed captioning feature when live streaming.
